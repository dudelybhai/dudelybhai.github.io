Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakes the world has ever known. Few have tried it and lived to tell about it, and these are their teststories.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs,sugar, and butter in a large bowl.
  2. Spread into a non-stick fish.
  3. Bake a 350 degrees for 1 hour.
  4. Eat and Enjoy!
